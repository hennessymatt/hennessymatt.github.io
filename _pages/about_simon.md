**Simon Finney**

Simon is developing models to understand the mechanical stresses experienced by hydrogel-coated stem cells in transit to the liver.  He is a DPhil student in Oxford co-supervised by [Sarah Waters](https://people.maths.ox.ac.uk/waters/Waters/Dr_Sarah_Waters.html) and [Andreas Münch](http://people.maths.ox.ac.uk/muench/).
